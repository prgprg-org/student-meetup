We are always looking for speakers to present at our meetups. If you are interested in giving a talk, please let us know at [pl-meetup@jakubdupak.com](mailto:pl-meetup@jakubdupak.com) or reach out on our Discord. We are happy to help out and support you, if you are unsure about any aspect of a talk.

### Time

Talks can take from 5 to 60 minutes. Most of our talks are about 40 minutes long.

### Topics

**We are open to any topic related to programming languages, compilers, and runtime systems. If you are unsure if your topic is a good fit, feel free to reach out to us. But so far we have never turned down a talk.**

You absolutelly don't need to be an expert in the topic you are presenting! You can review an interesting paper/article/blog post or pick a topic you would like to learn more about and have a discussion about it.

One of our organizers, Jakub Dupák, presented a talk about PDF. He knew nothing PDFs two weeks before the talk. So don't be afraid to pick any topic you are interested in!
